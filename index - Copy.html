About Director

Saravanan is the founder of Authi Realty. The company was started in 1998 with one goal in mind – to deliver a superior client experience. Saravanan focused on consistent performance and client satisfaction which remain the cornerstone of the company’s philosophy.

With a Master of Business Administration from Madurai Kamaraj University ,Saravanan brought a technical understanding of Real Estate Sales that allowed him to excel in his career as a Broker. Saravanan is able to see larger, global trends that exist in today’s complex world economy which results in accurate predictions and advice for his clients.

Saravanan has a true passion for real estate and home ownership. He has personally helped over 2,000 families with their real estate needs and has crossed over One Billion dollars in sales due to his creative marketing and ability to stay on the cutting edge of technology. Saravanan passionately believes in the product that he sells and his ability to help his customer's achieve their dreams of owning a home.

Saravanan has dedicated 20 years to the Real Estate Industry and continues to work with the same fervor as when he began his career. In his free time, Saravanan is a helping minded person.

Company Profile

Authi REALTY INDIA PVT. LTD. is a Real Estate Company having offices in Chennai offering Professional Services like Selling, Buying and Leasing of Commercial / Residential Properties in prime areas of the city.

The concept of offering Professional Real Estate Services in India emerged after the success of the firm . All our offices are located in prime areas of each city and are accessible. We employ highly educated and talented professionals. Our technical equipment are state of the art.
